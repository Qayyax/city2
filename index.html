Museum

The Peterborough Museum and Archives is a cultural landmark located at 300 Hunter Street East in Peterborough, Ontario. It is the repository for archaeological archives generated within the area administered by Peterborough City Council. The museum holds more than 2,000 different collections of original documentary materials, which include photographs, maps, and other historical documents. Admission to the museum is by donation.

Events and Festivals

Peterborough Music Fest

This is a free outdoor concert series held at Del Crary Park in downtown Peterborough. The festival features a wide range of musical genres and styles, from up-and-coming talents to past favorites and contemporary musicians

Peterborough Folk Festival

This festival celebrates folk music and culture and features local and international artists. It is held annually in August

Doors Open Peterborough

This event offers free tours of heritage buildings that are either not normally open to the public or would normally charge a fee for admittance. It provides a great way to learn about Peterborough's history.
